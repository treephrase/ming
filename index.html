About

The Eye
Behind the Lens

I am a photographer drawn to the edges of experience — the raw energy of a live concert, the delicate boundary between humanity and nature, and the split second before the world shifts.

My work lives between two worlds: the kinetic chaos of music photography and the quiet harmony of the natural world. I rarely direct my subjects; I let things unfold organically. I simply observe and record. My memory fades away easily, so I press the shutter to freeze time and recreate it, to simply forge a key to the past.
